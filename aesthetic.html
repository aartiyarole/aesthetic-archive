Know Your Aesthetic

Artcore is a vibrant, expressive aesthetic that combines visual art, surrealism, and an eclectic mix of styles.

Things to Look for

Color Pattern : Bright, Neon Colors ,Pastels with Bold Contrasts,Monochrome with Pops of Color,Artistic Prints

Unique Elements : Experimental Fashion , Playful Accessories , Makeup as Art.

Baddie Core is a bold, confident, and empowering aesthetic that embraces unapologetic self-expression. It’s all about exuding confidence and a fierce, high-maintenance attitude, while incorporating street style, glam, and a touch of luxury.

Things to Look for

Color Pattern : Neutrals & Earth Tones ,Bold, Statement Colors , Monochrome Looks , Metallic Accents

Unique Elements : Signature Hairstyling ,Statement Accessories, Street Chic , Attitude and Energy

Cottagecore is a romantic, nature-inspired aesthetic that emphasizes the simple pleasures of rural life and rural aesthetics. It celebrates the beauty of the countryside, organic farming, and rustic living, focusing on harmony with nature and a slower, more peaceful way of life.

Things to Look for

Color Pattern : Pastels & Soft Tones, Earthy and Natural , Floral Prints & Patterns , Warm Tones

Unique Elements : Nature-Inspired Clothing , Gardening & Farming Elements , Rustic & Vintage Decor , Wholesome Activities

Dark Academia is an aesthetic that revolves around intellectualism, the pursuit of knowledge, and a love for classical literature, philosophy, and the arts. It draws inspiration from gothic architecture, old universities, and the mystique of academic life.

Things to Look for

Color Pattern : Muted & Earthy Tones , Neutral & Dark Hues ,Accents of Gold & Brass ,Plaid & Tweed Patterns.

Unique Elements : Vinatage Clothing, Intellectual Hobbies , Moody , Academic Spaces, Romaticization of Melancholy , Historic Influence

The E-Girl aesthetic is a playful and rebellious subculture that emerged from the online world, particularly from social media platforms like TikTok, Instagram, and Twitch. It draws inspiration from a mix of alternative, punk, goth, and anime-inspired styles. E-Girls are known for their bold and expressive fashion, combining edgy streetwear with soft, whimsical elements.

Things to Look for

Color Pattern : Neon & Bright Colors , Pastels & Soft Tones ,Black & Dark Tones ,Metallics & Accents

Unique Elements : Bold Makeup Looks, Colored & Pastel Hair , Layered & Grunge-Inspired Fashion Anime & Cartoon References,Social Media & Gaming Culture:

The French It Girl aesthetic is synonymous with effortless, chic elegance. It is a style that has been admired for its ability to look fashionable without appearing overly styled. French It Girls, often seen as icons of modern Parisian style, have mastered the art of looking naturally put-together, with a focus on minimalist, yet stylish clothing and an air of nonchalance.

Things to Look for

Color Pattern : Neutral Tones , Pop of Red , Soft Pastels , Monochrome Outfits.

Unique Elements : The Little Black Dress , Parisian Scarf , Classic Footwear , The “No Makeup” Makeup Look , Vintage Inspired

The Girl Next Door aesthetic embodies a charming, approachable, and down-to-earth style. It’s all about that sweet, girl-next-door vibe, where fashion is both cute and relatable, blending simplicity with a touch of femininity. This aesthetic highlights a natural beauty that doesn’t rely on heavy makeup or extravagant clothing, making it feel authentic and effortlessly stylish.

Things to Look for

Color Pattern : Layered, Practical Outfits, Soft, Earthy Tones & Pastels ,Cute & Playful Accessories ,Preppy & Vintage Elements ,Soft Pastels

Unique Elements : Polished Casual Looks , Easy, Natural Makeup
